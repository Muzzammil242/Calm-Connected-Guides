Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Calm & Connected Guides ("we", "us", "our") handles personal information you provide when visiting our sales pages and purchasing our digital guides.

1. Who is the data controller

Muzzammil Sarwar (sole proprietor), trading as Calm & Connected Guides, House 639, Street 44, G-9/1, Islamabad 44000, Pakistan. Contact: muzzammil@umaish.com.

2. What information we collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to deliver the products you buy:

  • Order data — your name, email address, and country, provided to our payment platform Whop at checkout. We receive this information from Whop in order to deliver your purchase and answer support questions.
  • Email correspondence — if you email us, we store your message and reply so we can keep a record of the conversation.
  • Basic site analytics — anonymised page views and referral sources, used only to understand traffic. No individual user is identified or profiled.

We do not collect payment card details. All payment information is handled by Whop and never reaches our systems.

3. How we use your information

  • To deliver the digital guide you purchased.
  • To respond to questions or refund requests.
  • To send occasional product-related emails (only if you opt in at checkout or via a separate sign-up form).
  • To comply with legal and tax obligations.

4. Payment processing

All payments are processed by Whop, which acts as our checkout and payment platform. When you buy a product, Whop collects and stores your payment information under its own privacy policy: whop.com/privacy.

5. Email marketing

If you opt in to receive email updates, we may send infrequent emails about new guides or related content. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link at the bottom of every email.

6. Cookies

This site uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not run advertising or third-party tracking cookies on the sales pages. Whop's checkout page may set its own cookies for payment processing under its privacy policy.

7. Data retention

Order data is retained for as long as required by tax and accounting law (typically 7 years). Email correspondence is retained for up to 2 years unless you ask us to delete it sooner.

8. Your rights

Where applicable under GDPR or similar laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email muzzammil@umaish.com.

9. Children

Our products are not directed to people under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always published on this page with the "Last updated" date above.
